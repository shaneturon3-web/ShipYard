# WO-QPC-006 — AI assistance policy (Human)

## Public-facing

**NONE** unless legally required.

## Internal doctrine

AI supports clinicians operationally. AI does **not** replace therapy, judgment, diagnosis, or human care.

## Allowed (internal / PILOT)

- intake summarization  
- workflow assistance  
- reminder drafting  
- scheduling optimization  
- administrative support  
- documentation acceleration  

## Forbidden

- AI therapist framing  
- autonomous care  
- AI-led sessions  
- chatbot therapy positioning
