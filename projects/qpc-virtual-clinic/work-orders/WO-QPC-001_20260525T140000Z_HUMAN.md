# WO-QPC-001 — Executive architecture (Human)

## Objective

Deploy a clinically credible Quebec psychology clinic that feels human, reduces friction, appears operationally mature, and uses AI only as clinician support infrastructure.

## QPC is / is not

| QPC IS | QPC IS NOT |
|--------|------------|
| Human-to-human psychology clinic | AI clinic |
| Multicultural, trilingual, modern | Healthtech startup branding |
| Therapist as product surface | SaaS product publicly |
| Operationally organized, trustworthy | Automation-first marketing |

## Strategic separation

- **Public:** QPC = Quebec Psychology Clinic  
- **Internal:** PsyNova = modular operational infrastructure (intact; spare parts)

## Core ethos

> Human therapist helps the patient. AI helps the human psychologist.

Patient must feel: presence, empathy, organization, professionalism, continuity, accessibility.

Never: AI-first branding, “smart clinic”, futuristic identity signaling.

## Deployment principle

Do **not** rebuild operational commodities prematurely. Accelerate with Jane, Zoom, Stripe, WhatsApp, Google Workspace, Twilio, calendars, secure forms, multilingual workflows.

## Phases (honest labels)

| Phase | Scope |
|-------|--------|
| **NOW** | Human-first MVD — Next.js shell, wrapped SaaS, hidden AI |
| **PILOT** | Quiet analytics, admin summarization, clinician assistance |
| **FUTURE** | Internalize scheduling/orchestration only after stabilization |
